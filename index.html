Jon snow - The white wolf.

Jon Snow is the bastard son of Eddard Stark, Lord of Winterfell.[8] He has five half-siblings: Robb, Sansa, Arya, Bran, and Rickon Stark.

While Lord Eddard openly acknowledged Jon as his son and allowed him to live at Winterfell with his half-siblings, Jon felt like an outsider nonetheless. Jon was raised at Winterfell. At the age of fourteen, Jon joins the Night's Watch, where he earns the nickname Lord Snow.
